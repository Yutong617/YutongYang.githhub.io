Ragdoll--my favorite

Ragdoll cat is one of the most friendly animal in the world.

It like to follow breeder from one place to another, so people

also give the name "dog-like cats". Ragdolls come in six

different colors: red, seal, chocolate and the corresponding

"dilutes",including blue, lilac, and cream. Ragdolls have 3

different patterns--bicolor, mitted,colorpoint.
